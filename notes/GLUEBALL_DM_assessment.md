## 2. The honest correction: the miracle is partial — the dial is ξ, not α

The proposal's framing ("SIMP freeze-out lands on Ω_DM with no fine-tuning") holds for the *mass scale and
coupling*, but not for the *abundance*. Pure glue has no renormalizable portal, so the operative regime is
the **decoupled cannibal** one, where the relic is controlled by the hidden-to-visible temperature ratio ξ:

> **Sweeping α_eff across the entire natural range [0.5, 4π] moves the viable ξ by only ×1.11.**
> The abundance requires `ξ = 0.0062–0.0069` (Λ_h=30 MeV) / `0.0051–0.0057` (52) / `0.0040–0.0044` (105) —
> an ~11%-wide band, i.e. the inflaton branching to the hidden sector must be
> **`Br ≈ (0.4–3)×10⁻¹⁰`, selected to ~40%** (`Br ∝ ξ⁴`) — 3.5 decades above gravitational leakage
> (`Br_grav ≈ 7×10⁻¹⁴`), 10 decades below thermal.

So the ledger entry: **this proposal trades ADM's *explained* `Ω_DM/Ω_b ≈ 5` (ECCG's key virtue) for a
`Br ~ 2×10⁻¹⁰` reheating selection.** One dial for another — the new dial is arguably more natural (a tiny
branching to a portal-less sector is generic; a ×1.11 band within it is not), but it must be stated, not
hidden. The "why comparable to baryons" coincidence returns unexplained.
